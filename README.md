# 📊 Data Analyst Interview Prep — 15-Day Dashboard

> A fully interactive, browser-based study dashboard to prepare for Data Analyst interviews in 15 days — 30 to 60 minutes daily.

🔗 **Live Site:** [namratharao22.github.io/DA_Roadmap](https://namratharao22.github.io/DA_Roadmap/)

---

## 🗂 What's Inside

This dashboard is a single-file HTML app with three tabs:

### 📅 Roadmap Tab
- Structured 15-day study plan across 6 topic tracks
- Progress tracker with per-track completion counters
- Click any day card to open a detail panel with topics, practice tasks, and direct resource links
- Check off days as you complete them — progress saves in your browser automatically
- Filter by topic: SQL, Python, Stats, BI & Viz, Case Study, Behavioral

### 🔗 Resources Tab
- 40+ curated free and paid resources organized by track
- Covers LeetCode, StrataScratch, DataLemur, Kaggle, StatQuest, Mode Analytics, Tableau Public, Power BI, Exponent, and more
- Every resource is labeled **Free** or **Paid** with a direct link

### ❓ FAQ & Questions Tab
- 20+ most frequently asked interview questions with full structured answers
- Sections: SQL, Python/pandas, Statistics & A/B Testing, Product Analytics, Behavioral, and Trick Questions
- SQL answers include working code examples (window functions, CTEs, cohort queries, consecutive-day problems)
- Behavioral answers follow the STAR framework with coaching tips
- Accordion-style — click any question to expand the answer

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open the [live dashboard](https://namratharao22.github.io/DA_Roadmap/)
2. Start with **Day 1** and work through one card per day
3. Click a day card to see the full study plan, tasks, and resource links for that day
4. Check the ✓ button when done — your progress saves automatically in your browser
5. Use the **Resources** tab any time to find practice materials
6. Use the **FAQ** tab to review high-frequency interview questions before your interview
